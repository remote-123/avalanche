About

Take part in pre-sales with confidence or stake your SALE tokens to govern them and earn!
  • Teams can create and setup their token sales easily and conveniently
  • Users can participate in token sales through a secured DAPP smart contract using a wallet such as Metamask
  • Funding generated is locked in a smart contract and released in series to avoid exit scams
  • Funding and team tokens are released only when SALE token holders vote and agree for release
  • SALE stakers that vote with the majority will earn bonus cryptocurrency from the participating token sale
  • SALE stakers that vote with the minority will have 10% of their staked tokens burned

Platform


dApp cross-chain SALE Platform

Our dApp will eventually be a platform that can be integrated into any decentralized exchange across the cryptocurrency industry. With this decentralized exchanges will have their own IEO platform.


Platform as a Service

The Launchpad Plugin will be an easy to plug integration service that any decentralized exchange can use to onboard DxSALE's governance services. After integration the DEX will have the option to use their own native token in conjunction with SALE tokens for governance.

High Level Features

Secured Token Sales

No more exit scams or rug pulls
Token sale is automatically generated on desired blockchain using secured smart contracts. Funding is divided into liquidity and series funding.

DeFi Governance

More power to the community
SALE token stakers govern release of funding. When a teams series of funding is due for release, SALE token holders that staked on the pre-sale can vote to release or withold the funds.

Decentralized Hosting

100% uptime
Hosted as a DAPP on several blockchain platforms for 100% availability and reliability. Multiple domains to access the DAPP will be provided to ensure uninterupted uptime

Staking, Voting and Burn

Stake and vote with SALE tokens
Stakers that vote with the majority earn rewards. Stakers that vote with the minority have 10% of their tokens burned.

Simple and Cost Effective

Token Sales with Peace of Mind
Teams looking to offer tokens can use the platform simply by connecting to the DAPP. The only fees that teams need to pay are the gas fees required to run the contract

PaaS to all DEX

DAPP that any DEX can integrate
The DAPP is a Smart Contract that is accessible through any interface. Any DEX can integrate the smart contract and add their own overlay UI to use as a decentralized IEO platform.
